Hot stone massage therapy: Shila Abhyanga

Hot stone massage, the ancient Ayurvedic technique that uses herbal Ayurvedic oils and basalt stones heated in water to give a slow, deep tissue massage.

This massage is traditionally given to stimulate the circulatory system, soften and relax the muscles, release toxins, relieve pains and muscle spasms, and induce a state of deep relaxation.
